How bad is the quality of air in Malaysia?

The country of Malaysia is found in southeast Asia, made up of thirteen states and three federal territories.
These are separated by the south china sea into two separate sections, with peninsular Malaysia being the region that it typically associated with the countries name, hosting the capital city of Kuala Lumpur. The other region is located in Borneo, being known as the eastern portion of Malaysia respectively. There are some noticeable discrepancies in the levels of pollution between the two regions. In 2019 Malaysia came in ranked 50th place amongst all the countries of the world, with a PM2.5 rating of 19.36 µg/m³, putting its yearly average into the moderately polluted range.
